How to work

Consumers

  • Order your favorite products from favorite farmers!
  • Receive the products delivered to you!
  • Review the product, farmer, and LocalFarmers team!

Local Farmers

  • Verify farmers and products!
  • Publish farmer and product details!
  • Consolidate orders from customers!
  • Arrange for supply by farmers!
  • Procure, segregate, and deliver to customers!
  • Get customers' reviews on products and service!

Farmers

  • Produce healthy farm products free from harmful chemicals!
  • Publish your product details and prices!
  • Get orders from customers!
  • Supply to consumers directly or through LocalFarmers team!
  • Receive customer reviews on your products!
