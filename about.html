ABOUT FRESH FARMING

AGRICULTURE

Fresh farming is a term referring to the direct sale of farm-fresh produce, typically fruits and vegetables, directly from the farmer to the consumer, often bypassing traditional retail channels. This approach emphasizes freshness, quality, and direct farmer-consumer relationships.

Fresh farming likely refers to farm-fresh products, emphasizing the direct connection between the farm and the consumer. This could include a variety of food items like fruits, vegetables, grains, and even processed products like cereals and noodles, all sourced directly from farms. The focus is on fresh, unpreserved food, often with an emphasis on local sourcing and sustainability.
